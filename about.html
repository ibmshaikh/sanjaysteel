Sanjay Steel company



Sanjay Steel Company and Sanjay Ispat Alloys was established way back in 1960 first in the name of Engineering Euqipment Co. by one of the most experienced person Mr. Vrajlal U. Jhaveri.
Some of our widely accepted products include Tool steels, Die steels, Carbon steels, Alloy steels, High Speed steels and many more. We supply these products on the basis of special industrial requirements of our clients.
We are committed to elevate the standards of our products and services to offer customer satisfaction through excellence in quality. Every employee is dedicated toward this aim and every measure and care is taken to ensure production of superior products, quality packaging, timely delivery, competent prices and excellent after sales service.
