Trapped Drill Rod


Brief introduction

1. Raw material: It is 55SiMnMo or 95CrMo of 320*480 large-section contunuous casting billets, then rolling into 150*150 square billets after inserting core rods and seamless pipes.
2. Production process: involving multiple processes, such as steel rolling, forging, normalization, quenching and rempering, etc.
3. Shank size: h22*108mm/H25*108mm/H25*159mm.
4. Angle: 7/11/12 degree.
5. Package: 5 pieces with a samll bundle,50 or 100 pieces with a big bundle.


Quality Level

According to dffrent choices of customers, we offer four quality, uncluding Grade A, Grade B, Grade C, Grade D. Customers can choose suitable drill roads according to diffrent hardness of rock and various types of rock drilling machines. Grade A drill road QSAAA and GTIII can be applied to solid rocks. III level or above f>=12 rocks according to Protodikonow's Hardness Coefficient of Rock. Grade B drill road QSAA abd G30 is suitable for IV level or above and f>=12 rocks. Grade C and Grade D drill road suitable for IV level or below and f<=8 rocks.
